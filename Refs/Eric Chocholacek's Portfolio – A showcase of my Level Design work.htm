Portfolio Projects

Below are what I feel are currently my best projects/levels. To see more projects and in-depth information, click on any below or visit the Portfolio section of the website.

The Megastructure

3rd Person Action-Adventure Blockout [UE4]

Taking inspiration from a manga series I enjoy (Blame!), I tried to create a vast, maze-like, dark, decrepit structure that encouraged exploration with rewards and unlockable areas, but also had an intense atmosphere that kept Players on their toes while avoiding dangerous entities.

CGMA Level Design Projects

Collection of 3rd Person Levels [UE4]

A collection of the projects I completed for the Level Design for Games course at CGMA. Each week, I was tasked to create a new or iterate on a previous level to explore the lessons taught in the lectures that were presented that week.

Level Study of Death Stranding

Open World Level Blockout [UE4]

A study of the level design and mechanics of the video game "Death Stranding" created by Kojima Productions. Built during a 48-hour "level design jam", this is a short, single-player experience set in a large canyon where the Player must traverse challenging landscapes and sneak past deadly entities to collect Cargo from a dangerous enemy encampment in order to return it to an NPC at a safe area.

Fortune

3rd Person Level Blockout [UE4]

A Third-Person Co-opetition Shooter game set in a world influenced by Westerns and H.P. Lovecraft, pitting Players against players and other unspeakable horrors as they hunt for valuable relics of unknown worlds.
(Re-imagining of Left Behind)

Split/Edit

1st Person Horror Game [UE4]

A First-Person Atmospheric Horror game with some light puzzle elements set within a seemingly abandoned mansion that used to be home to 2 deranged scientists and their son. Players wake up in this haunted house surrounded by strange video equipment and must use VHS tapes to find a way to overcome the horrors they will face.
